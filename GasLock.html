GasLock

Gas leaks pose a threat to households and businesses, leading to potential injuries that leads to death . Existing gas detection systems often lack the ability to automatically shut off the gas source in the event of a leak, leaving occupants vulnerable to the dangers of the gases exposure .

Our solution is a Gas leak detection and automatic closing system that provides immediate reaction when detecting any leaks. Our solution ensures fast action to prevent accidents, protect lives, and safeguard property from the devastating consequences of gas leaks .
